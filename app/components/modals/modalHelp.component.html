Instructions for graphs

The below graph represents how you ranked yourself in all 15 TFL Dimensions. 20% represents that you rated yourself not satisfied and 100% indicates that you ranked yourself completely satisfied. It is through self-awareness that we can celebrate the areas of our lives in which we are satisfied and begin taking mindful action in the dimensions we want to change.

There are 4 additional grayed out words below the chart: Importance, Motivation, Happiness and Performance. How you ranked yourself in each area will populate once you click each word. We encourage you to click and un-click each area to compare and contrast your results.

Click on the Take Action button and the dimensions you ranked the greatest motivation to change will populate. We encourage you to focus your efforts on the things that are important to you so take a look at your results and we hope you are motivated to take action.

The colors below denote which each item on the charts mean:

  • Blue: How satisfied are you with this dimension in your life?
  • Green: How important is it for your to gain greater balance and satisfaction in this dimension of your life
  • Black: How motivated are your to improve this dimension in your life?
  • Orange: How much is your overall happiness affected?
  • Purple: How much is your performance at work or school affected?

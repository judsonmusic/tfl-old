Instructions for graphs

The Train For Life graph represents how you ranked yourself in all 15 TFL Dimensions. 20% represents that you rated yourself not satisfied and 100% indicates that you ranked yourself completely satisfied. It is through self-awareness that you can celebrate the areas of your life in which you are satisfied as well as begin taking mindful action in the dimensions you want to change.

There are 4 additional grayed out words below the chart: Importance, Motivation, Happiness and Performance. How you ranked yourself in each area will populate once you click each word. We encourage you to click and un-click each area to compare and contrast your results.

Click on Your TFL Take Action button and the dimensions you ranked the greatest motivation to change will populate. We encourage you to focus your efforts on the things that are important to you so take a look at your results and we hope you are motivated to take action.

The colors below denote which each item on the charts mean:

  • This dimension of my life is important to me.
  • This dimension negatively affects my happiness.
  • Performing to the best of my ability is impacted by this dimension.
  • I am satisfied with this dimension of my life.
  • I am actively working to improve and/or maintain this dimension of my life.
  • I am motivated to improve and/or maintain this area of my life.

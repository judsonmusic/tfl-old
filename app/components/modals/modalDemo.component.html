Welcome to Your TFL Dashboard!

Welcome to Your Train For Life Dashboard. You now have access to interactive reports which will provide you a comprehensive overview of how you rated yourself in 15 dimensions of your life. Begin unlocking each dimension by clicking on the icon.

Knowing where you are and what is important to you is the first step towards greater self-awareness, so we encourage you to use your TFL Dashboard to help you make mindful decisions regarding your total wellbeing.

You have the ability to change the course of your life one dimension at a time.

Once you unlock all 15 dimensions you will have access to your TFL Tool Kit:

  • Click the Icon and reveal the dimensions you are most motivated to take action.
  • Click the Icon and view how you rated all 15 dimensions at a glance.
  • Click the Icon and print your comprehensive TFL Empowerment Guide which gives you information on all 15 dimensions.

NOTE : You can opt to unlock all your dimensions at once or you can take your time and go back to the existing user section on the home page and pick up where you left off.

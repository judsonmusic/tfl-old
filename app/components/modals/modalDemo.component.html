Welcome to Your TFL Dashboard!

Welcome to Your Train For Life Dashboard. You now have access to interactive reports which will provide you a comprehensive overview of how you rated yourself in 15 dimensions of your life. Begin unlocking each dimension by clicking on the icon.

Knowing where you are and what is important to you is the first step towards greater self-awareness, so we encourage you to use your TFL Dashboard to help you make informed decisions regarding your total wellbeing.

You have the ability to change the course of your life one dimension at a time.

Once you unlock all 15 dimensions you will be able to click the Icon which will display the dimensions you are most interested in taking action.

NOTE : You can opt to unlock all your dimensions at once or you can take your time and log back in later and pick up where you left off.

Welcome to Your TFL Dashboard!

Welcome to Your TFL Results. Your customized Train For Life Dashboard will display interactive reports which will provide you a comprehensive overview of how you rated yourself in 15 dimensions of your life. Your charts and graphs will display how satisfied and important each dimension is to you, your level of motivation to change and how your happiness and your school and/or job performance are impacted. Knowing where you are and what is important to you is the first step towards greater self-awareness. We encourage you to use your TFL Dashboard to help you make informed decisions regarding your total wellbeing.

You have the ability to change the course of your life one dimension at a time.

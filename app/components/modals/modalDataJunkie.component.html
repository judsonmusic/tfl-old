Data Junkie

The following interactive TFL Graph will provide you a comprehensive overview of how you ranked yourself in all 15 dimensions of your life. Each bar on the graph is color coded and represents the following:

  • How important the dimension is to you
  • How the dimension impacts your happiness
  • How the dimension impacts your performance
  • How satisfied you are with the dimension.
  • How actively you are working to improve and/or maintain the dimension
  • How motivated you are to improve and/or maintain the dimension

Note: The way you responded to the above questions are reflected on the graph from a low of 20% to a high of 100%

You can populate the graph by clicking the grayed out words below the chart: Importance, Happiness, Performance, Satisfaction, Action, and Motivation. Remember how you ranked yourself in each dimension will populate once you click the corresponding word below the chart. You can also click and un-click each word in order to populate or suppress the 6 bars in each dimension. This interactive view will allow you to compare and contrast each dimension in all 6 areas.

By recognizing what impacts your total wellbeing you can begin to take action in the dimensions which are most important to you. Have fun reviewing your data and remember self-awareness is the first step towards personal empowerment.

Instructions: You can click on the Icon at any time for instructions on how to use what is displayed on your dashboard.

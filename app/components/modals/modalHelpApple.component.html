Instructions for graphs

Taking action requires awareness and desire, so take a look at which dimensions you ranked you were most motivated to change.

Your TFL circle graphs reflect:

  • Blue: How satisfied are you with this dimension in your life?
  • Red: How important is it for your to gain greater balance and satisfaction in this dimension of your life
  • Green: How motivated are your to improve this dimension in your life?
  • Yellow: How much is your overall happiness affected?
  • Orange: How much is your performance at work or school affected?

You can mouse over each ring in the circle to view your percentage of satisfaction in each area. 20% represents least satisfied and 100% represents completely satisfied.

We encourage you to focus your efforts and to take action in the areas that have been personally selected for you.

Click the Get Informed Icon to learn more about each dimension and to gain immediate access to tools and resources which will help you with Your TFL Action Plan

You will notice a View All TFL Results button if you would like to see each dimension displayed in this circle graph format.
